Abel

Abel is a familiar that shot once per second in rebirth. It was also on the oposite side of Isaac and shot oposite from where isaac shot. This was obviously awfull for an item. You would also find the item alot in golden chests. This item nowadays is a part of conjoined, but it wasnt back in rebirth. In repentance, it got changed to shoot towards Issac regardless of where he was shooting, making it slightly better, and removing it from the chest pools. Just to be clear, slighty does not mean good, oh no, its still horrible, but now you have atleast some fringe cases where it can be useful.
