The binding of isaac

The first boss that got infamous is probably "The Bloat". This boss got so infamous to the point that someone made reddit page of the boss called "Fuck Bloat" which is a site where people complain about the boss, more or less.
The reason for its hate is that you could not stand in front of it or on the sides of it, otherwise you would get blasted by his brimstone attack. This also creates a problem when you first get into the boss room and are either to the side the bottom On top of that, he has two eyes that keeps going around the room, making the whole fight very hectic.
