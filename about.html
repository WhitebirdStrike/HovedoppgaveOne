About

The Binding of Isaac is a top-down rougelike game which we controll a kid called Isaac as we travel through a hellscape of enemies to get to the end. This game is hugely inspired by The Legend of Zelda by its form of the rooms, items and enemies. The difference here is that its mature content, which has gotten it a rating of 18+. While it might not be the most violent game out there, it still has it good share of disturbing content, like references to things on the internet which you should not search up, poop and piss related items, disturbing enemies, and blood and gore. The game has a huge selection of items that interact and work together to make synergies The fun of the game comes from having every run be different.
